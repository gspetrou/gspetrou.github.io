Whats up.

Glad to see you made it here! Take off your shoes, grab a blanket, and let me tell you a little bit about myself. My name is George Petrou, I'm an undergraduate studying Computer Engineering at Penn State University. Born and raised in New Jersey you can often find me hiking, longboarding, lifting, and tucked away in a dark room somewhere programming on a nice afternoon. Writing code and working with hardware are my primary crafts. The hardware end of things is where I can apply my knowledge of physics and the world around us to create a machine, a tool which helps improve our everyday lives. The software end of things is where I can really push these machines to their limits and give a brain to an otherwise dull object. This unity of software and hardware, the virtual and physical worlds, is what interests me and drives me to lead a career in Computer Engineering.
