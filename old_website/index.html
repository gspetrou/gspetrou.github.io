What's up.

Glad to see you made it here! My name is George Petrou but I won't bore you with the details you can simply read above. I was born in New Jersey and raised in my hometown of Holmdel with an older brother and twin sister. Ever since I can remember I've always had somewhat of an affinity for electronics. How small lines of metal connecting curious pieces of black and green plastic could accomplish anything blew my mind. This has and continues to peak my interest. That is why I've chosen a career path beginning with Computer Engineering. It is the unity of software, the virtual "brain" behind the machine, and the hardware, the physical "being" of the machine that I find fascinating to explore, create, and learn from.

Apart from electronics I really enjoy hiking, longboarding, running, collecting vinyl records, and eating food, no matter the kind. I'm also an audiohead with music tastes ranging from Hozier, Tame Impala, and the Alabama Shakes to Frank Ocean, Daniel Caesar, and Omar Apollo. If you're into any of the things I just mentioned, or if you want to chat about something else feel free to contact me. I wont bite! (I promise).
